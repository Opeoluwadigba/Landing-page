SpaceX's Starlink internet service

This page is a brief introduction to the internet service provided by Elon Musk's SpaceX known as starlink.

Starlink was created by Elon Musk to solve the problem of slow internet connections and high latency. So far customers have found this problems to have been solved, which has garnered a lot of praise for the service.

Availabilty

Starlink is currently available in the following countries: Nigeria, Sweden, Finland, Norway, Iceland, Denmark, Latvia among other places.

Available plans

Starlinks available plans include:

• Residential

• Business

• Roam

• Maritime

• Aviation

What to expect from starlink

Starlink offers much faster upload and download speeds, with greatly reduced latency. One should expect speeds between 50 and 200Mbps, though you could have lower speeds due to connectivity issues and/or bad weather.
